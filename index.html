Bethlehem

Bethlehem is a historic city located in the West Bank, Palestine, about 10 kilometers south of Jerusalem. It is most famous as the birthplace of Jesus Christ, making it a significant pilgrimage site for Christians. The Church of the Nativity, a UNESCO World Heritage site, marks the traditional spot of Jesus' birth and draws visitors from around the world. Bethlehem is also known for its rich cultural heritage, with a blend of Palestinian traditions, religious significance, and ancient history, making it a key center of faith and tourism in the region.
